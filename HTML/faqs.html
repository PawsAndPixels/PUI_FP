Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept requests for custom pieces?
+
Yes! I am open to creating custom pieces based on your preferences and ideas. Whether it's a unique design, a personalized item, or a special request, feel free to reach out, and we can discuss the details to bring your vision to life.
Do you make keychains?
+
Currently, I do craft keychains, and they are available for purchase. However, please note that I may not continue to produce keychains in the future. If you're interested in acquiring one, it's a good idea to check out the available designs now.
How do you decide on themes for your designs?
+
My creative process is inspired by the simple joys of everyday life. Whether it's nature, experiences, or small moments that bring happiness, these elements often influence and shape the themes of my designs. This ensures that each piece carries a unique and positive essence.
Do you make seasonal and/or holiday collections?
+
While I may not currently have specific seasonal or holiday collection, I'm actively exploring ideas and possibilities for introducing such sections in the future. Stay tuned for festive and themed creations that could make perfect gifts for special occasions!
If my piece breaks, how can I fix it?
+
Accidents happen, and if your piece happens to break, it can be challenging to restore it to its original state. However, one potential solution is to use epoxy glue, which can effectively bond the broken parts. While it might not be perfect, it can provide a reasonable fix to keep enjoying your cherished item.
Do you sell bulk?
+
Certainly! If you're interested in purchasing items in larger quantities, whether for events, gifts, or any other purpose, I offer bulk options. Feel free to inquire about the products you're interested in, and we can discuss pricing and customization options for bulk orders to meet your specific needs.
