Maker's Story

KikiReadsComics is a shop that embodies my passion for creative expression and love of all things cute! Titled after my nickname, Kiki, it is a place where I can share my polymer clay creations with the world.

I first began making kawaii charms with polymer clay five years ago, but at the time I wasn't making them to sell. Polymer clay was just something that made me happy and kept me busy. As I got better at it, my friends began to ask me if I had an Etsy and if I would be willing to sell some of my creations to them. Hence, KikiReadsComics was born! I love the experience I have selling online, and now I am able to sell at local craft fairs!

There's a lot of hard work that goes into producing high quality charms and packaging them for delivery. However, it's been so much fun and I'm thankful for the experience. By running this shop I want to use my creations to spread happiness around the world, one charm at a time!!
